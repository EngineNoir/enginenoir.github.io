URL

A URL, Uniform Resource Locator, is the link to a given website. Moreso it navigates to specific webpages of a given website. A URL consists of a few specific parts. Let us consider the following:

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Main_Page

The components of a URL are as follows:

  • Protocol: https://
  • Subdomain: en
  • Domain: wikipedia.org
  • Page: /wiki/Main_Page

Take note: wiki is the folder within which Main_Page resides!
